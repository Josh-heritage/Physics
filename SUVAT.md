### Using SUVAT to calculate displacement

Displacement, S, can be calculated from the SUVAT equations.

The equations for s are,
$s = vt - \frac{1}{2}at^2$ 
$s = ut + \frac{1}{2}at^2$
$s = \frac{t}{2}(u+v)$
$s=\frac{u^2+v^2}{2a}$
